**What is Hyperdash?**

Hyperdash is an advanced trading terminal and explorer for trading on Hyperliquid. Hyperdash allows you to dive into traders' performance, analyze additional data on the available markets and track, copy or counter trade wallets trading on Hyperliquid, through the publicly visible on-chain nature of the underlying protocol.

**Core contributors**

Hyperdash was acquired by [pvp.trade](http://pvp.trade) and innovated upon to create a new trading terminal with copy trading functionality and advanced tools for traders.
