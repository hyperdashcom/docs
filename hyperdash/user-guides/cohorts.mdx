Hyperdash aggregates and displays real time data on traders in the trading terminal for market insights

### **Cohorts**

View the current notional and absolute trader Long to Short ratio and Realised to Unrealised PnL ratio of traders on any pair, segmented based on PnL of traders or wallet size

You can also explore each cohort for a wider overview of their positioning across all major pairs, and their Long to Short ratio graphically over different time frames. Also included is a breakdown of the traders comprised within each cohort.

### Top Traders

See how top traders on each pair are positioned, including their entries, live liquidations levels, unrealised PnL and funding payments. You can filter for traders that are long or short with associated entries and notional trade size.

### Position Changes

See how traders positions change over various time frames, including their previous and new position size. This is useful to see which traders are responsible for large recent moves that may be affecting the market.

### Active TWAPs

Displays traders with active TWAP orders on the pair you are viewing, with the total order size, remaining size to deploy, time left and total progress to date.
