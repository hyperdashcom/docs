Hyperdash aggregates informative exchange metrics and insights for traders to use.

**Liquidation Heatmap**

- Filter by listed asset and time frame to see key levels of liquidations (available in 3D, 1W, 2W and 1M timeframes).

**Stop Loss Heatmap**

- Filter by listed asset and time frame to see key stop loss levels (available in 3D, 1W, 2W and 1M timeframes).

**Funding Rate / Countdown**

Filter between 7D and 30D on any pair to see graphically how the funding rate changes over time.

### Live Feeds

Instead of the Orderbook, traders can choose to see a view of live trades or live liquidations, filterable by minimum size to view.
